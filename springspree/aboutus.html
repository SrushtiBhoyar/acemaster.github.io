SpringSpree 2015



SpringSpree, the annual cultural festival of NIT Warangal, was commenced in 1978 as a requisite break for the students from enervating and monotonous class schedules and didatic courses. 36 years on, it hs today been established as one of the best cultural festivals in the nation enticing massive participation from the very center to the remote corners. Over the years, we have had famous and indigeneous artists, highlighting the festival with thier esteemed presence and their majestic performance

This year we hope to elevate our festival to a new attitude of unprecedented success that could be perpetuated pernnially ensure the entailment of the very intricate elements of art and culture, suffused along with the broader aspects and expand its outreach internationally, we have conjured with the theme WORLD FEST. The theme aims at incorporating the resplendent heritage of different parts of the world into the Indian environment and also bestow the students with a global outlook



National Institute of Technology, Warangal





Since its inception in the year 1959 as Regional Engineering College Warangal, NIT Warangal has culminated into a premier technical institute into a premier technical institut in India, with its alumini contributing profoundly to the engineering industry as well as ascending to unprecedented hieghts in finance, arts culture, politics and various other professional disciplines. At NIT Warangal, the faculty and the students strive towards not only emulating but exceeding the legacy of the alumini and the institute by imaculately professing towards the idea of holistic development of students. Along with their core and rudimentary engineering disciplines, the concepts of art, culture and heritage also have a profuse impact on the lives of the students pursuing their education at this institute of importance
